Careers with Acquire Power Occupational Therapy

Want to work in a thriving environment where you can learn, grow and make a real difference to peoples lives?

We are looking for self-motivated, organised Occupational Therapist to predominantly work with NDIS and Home Care package participants.
At APOT, we are passionate about helping our participants and our team-mates. We will guide, support and mentor you to through your journey with Acquire Power Occupational Therapy.

We have multiple full-time, part-time, casual roles. The position is open for skill set ranging from graduate Occupational Therapist to Grade 3 Occupational Therapist.

About you

1. You must be either in the final year of the Occupational Therapy bachelors course or a qualified Occupational Therapist.
2. You must be passionate about helping others, think out of box and love challenges
3. You must be a good team player and have a passion for learning
4. You must have good time management skills to be able to work on multiple cases
5. Must have an Australian drivers license
6. Be willing to travel locally and willing to use your car


Why work with us

1. We love sharing our knowledge and will appreciate your input
2. Opportunity to learn and work on a variety of complex cases
3. You will be fully supported and mentored during your time with us
4. We will help you to set your career plan and help you achieve it
5. We offer professional development courses relevant to the role
6. You will actively participate in clinical discussions, case studies with evidence-based practice
7. We offer flexible working hours
