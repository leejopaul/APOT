Services

Most of our services are conducted at your residence

Seating

Do you spend a long time in a chair and find it difficult to get on and off the chair?
Is your carer struggling to assist you?

We offer specialised seating assessment and a prescription for you and your carer's needs

Aids & Equipment Prescription

Do you require increased care needs due to the progressive nature of your condition?
Do you need equipments for your ongoing needs?

We provide assessment and prescription of aids and equipment such as hoist, slings, electric scooter, electric bed.

Chronic Disease Management

Do you have a chronic health condition?
Do you find day to day activities difficult?

We assess and recommend aids and equipment to enable you to engage in meaningful activities, maintain valued life roles and participate in the community.

Pressure Care Management

Are you at risk of developing pressure injury or have you had a history of pressure injury?
Does your carer find it challenging to change your position to prevent skin breakdown?
Are you looking to manage these problems with less manual handling?
Does your current seating system affect your posture and comfort?

We provide pressure injury assessment, liaise with external stakeholders. Prescribe appropriate equipment such as cushion and pressure care mattress to promote healing.

Wheelchair

Are you looking for a mobility device?
Are you housebound due to lack of a mobility device?
Do you have concerns about your posture?

We perform a detailed assessment around of needs and your carers needs. We provide an assessment of your seating requirement, including the prescription of electric and manual wheelchairs. We also offer complex seating assessment and recommendations to support your posture and your physiological needs.

Home Modification

Do you live with a mobility device?
Do you for your carer find it hard to access specific sections of the house?

We provide home modification services to make your house accessible. The recommendations are based on Australian standards and national construction code (NCC). The modifications include structural and non-structural changes including bathroom, access and kitchen modifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are you a registered NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme) provider?

    Yes, we are a registered NDIS provider and provide services to the participants predominantly in the south-eastern suburbs of Melbourne in Victoria.

  • How do I seek services from you?

    If you require services from us, please contact us via phone, fax or email. Details are listed in the Contact Us section of the website. We will get in touch with you to discuss your needs at the earliest convenient time. If you have a support coordinator, they too can get in touch with us to coordinate the service.

  • Do you need a copy of my NDIS plan?

    Yes, our preference is to obtain your NDIS plan. The plan provides us with the details, which helps us to prioritise your goals based on the available funding.

  • Regions covered?

    We service most suburbs of Melbourne.

  • How to make a referral and what to expect

    • To obtain service from us, please fill the online referral form <>
    • One of our friendly team members will get in touch with you to discuss your referral in detail and will offer an appointment.
    • You will receive a welcome package shortly after the appointment is scheduled. The welcome kit contains document such as privacy policy, fees, terms and conditions etc. We expect you to read and sign the documents prior to our visit.
    • During the appoinment we will assess the function and set goals with you and we will support and assist you to reach your goals.

  • How long do I have to wait receive a service

    We offer our services usually under two weeks. You case can be prioritised if required.
